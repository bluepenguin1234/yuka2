Know before it's in your cart.

Mamama scans any product barcode and gives a clear pregnancy or breastfeeding safety verdict in seconds — with the clinical reasoning behind it, tuned to your exact trimester.

How it works

  • Scan a barcode — food, drinks, skincare, cosmetics.
  • See the verdict — not recommended, use caution, no flags found, or limited data. We never overclaim.
  • Read the why — every ingredient rating cites sources like MotherToBaby, NIH LactMed, ACOG, and the FDA.
  • Stage-aware — verdicts shift as pregnancy progresses and once nursing begins.
