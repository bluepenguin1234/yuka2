Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-07-13

Mamama ("we," "us") is built on a simple position: your pregnancy is not a data product. This policy explains what the app handles, what we store, and your choices.

The short version

  • By default, your scans and profile stay on your device. Without an account, we hold no personal data about you.
  • We show no third-party ads and use no third-party marketing or tracking SDKs.
  • Health-context information (your stage, your scans) is never sold and never shared with advertisers or data brokers.

Information handled on your device

  • Your stage and optional due date — stored locally to compute stage-appropriate verdicts.
  • Scan history — stored locally (last 200 scans). You can clear it anytime (Profile → Clear scan history).

Information sent off your device

  • Barcode lookups. When you scan, the barcode is sent to the public Open Food Facts / Open Beauty Facts APIs to retrieve product data. These requests contain no name, email, or account identifier and are subject to those projects' terms.
  • Coverage reports. If a barcode isn't in our database, we log the barcode number and your stage (e.g. "trimester 2") anonymously — with no user identifier — so we know which products to cover next.

If you create an account (optional)

Accounts exist only to back up and sync your data. If you create one, we store: your email address, your stage/due date, your premium status, and your scan history, secured in our database (hosted on Supabase). We use this data solely to provide sync — nothing else.

Deletion: Profile → Delete account permanently deletes your account and all synced data, immediately, in-app. Local data is cleared at the same time.

Payments

Premium subscriptions are processed by Apple. We never see or store your payment details.

Photos (AI label reader, premium — when available)

If you photograph an ingredient label for analysis, the image is sent to our server and an AI provider (Anthropic) solely to produce the analysis, then discarded. Images are not used to train models and are not stored by us.

Children

Mamama is intended for adults and is not directed at children under 13.

Changes

We'll update this page and the effective date when the policy changes. Material changes will be announced in the app.

Contact

Questions or requests: support@mamama.app
