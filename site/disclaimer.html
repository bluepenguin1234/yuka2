Medical Disclaimer

Effective date: 2026-07-13

Mamama is an informational tool. It is not a medical device, does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment, and is not a substitute for the judgment of a qualified healthcare professional.

Verdicts shown in the app summarize published guidance from sources including MotherToBaby (the Organization of Teratology Information Specialists), the NIH Drugs and Lactation Database (LactMed), the American College of Obstetricians and Gynecologists (ACOG), the U.S. Food and Drug Administration (FDA), the UK National Health Service (NHS), and the European Food Safety Authority (EFSA). Evidence about exposures in pregnancy and lactation is often limited, and reputable sources sometimes disagree. Where they disagree, Mamama deliberately displays the more cautious rating.

  • A verdict of "No flags found" means no ingredient in the product matched a known concern in our database for your selected stage. It is not a guarantee of safety.
  • A verdict of "Limited data" means we could not verify enough of the product's ingredients to reach a confident assessment.
  • Product formulations change. Always check the physical label.

Always consult your doctor, midwife, or pharmacist before making decisions about foods, cosmetics, supplements, or medications during pregnancy or while breastfeeding. Never delay, disregard, or discontinue professional medical advice because of information in this app.

If you believe you have been exposed to something harmful, contact your healthcare provider immediately. In the United States, you can also call Poison Control at 1-800-222-1222.
